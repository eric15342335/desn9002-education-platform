Background

There are significant information gaps for underprivileged students, especially concerning school career planning. Additionally, the young generation lacks an inclusive networking, coaching, and mentorship platform that caters to their diverse needs.

Our Impact

Market Demand and Pain Points

Information Asymmetry: Young individuals seeking career development advice, support, and networks often face challenges due to a lack of accessible resources and guidance. This platform aims to bridge that gap by providing tailored support and connections.

Explore Solutions

Key Features

  • Resources Cloud Platform: Matches students with college and education planning counselors based on their academic performance, financial capacity, career goals, and overseas studying options.
  • Counselors Profiles: Detailed profiles for counselors including their qualifications, experience, and user reviews.
  • User Reviews & Ratings: Enables students to review and rate their counselors to ensure quality and reliability.
  • Community Forum: A space for students to connect, share experiences, and seek advice from peers and mentors.

Revenue Model

  • Commission for Matching: A fee charged for successfully matching students with counselors.
  • Platform Premium Fee: Subscription fees for premium features and enhanced access within the platform.
